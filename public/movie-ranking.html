The BEST Movies According to Melanie Garza

My top three favorite movies

The Phantom of the Opera

This is one of my absolute favorite movies. This is the first movie I watched that sparked my love for the theater I could watch this movie every day for the rest of my life.

Spiderman Into the Spiderverse

Being a mom of three, two of these lovelys being boys who are in love with spider man, this is probably my favorite spiderman movie ever made. I love marvel movies and continue to watch them all the time, and my boys favorite super hero is spiderman, I couldnt get them to stop watching this movie and it became one of my favorites

Sleeping Beauty

This Disney Classic has been one of my absolute favorites since I was a child, there is just something about watching a classic Disney princess movie that makes you feel all warm and fuzzy and makes you happy as you remeber your favorite childhood classics.
